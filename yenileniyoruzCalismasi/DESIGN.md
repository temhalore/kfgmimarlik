# Design System Document

This document outlines the core aesthetic and functional principles of our design system.

## Brand Identity

Our brand identity is built on a foundation of sophisticated simplicity and modern elegance.

### Color Palette

-   **Primary Color**: `#11467c` - A deep, authoritative blue, evoking trust and stability.
-   **Secondary Color**: `#A68966` - A warm, earthy tone that provides a balanced contrast.
-   **Tertiary Color**: `#D4AF37` - A rich, golden yellow for highlights and accents, suggesting quality and distinction.
-   **Neutral Color**: `#2C2E30` - A deep, near-black for backgrounds and foundational elements, ensuring readability and focus.

### Typography

-   **Headlines**: `spaceGrotesk` - Modern and distinctive for strong visual impact.
-   **Body Text**: `manrope` - Clean, highly legible, and versatile for continuous reading.
-   **Labels**: `manrope` - Clear and concise for UI elements and interactive components.

## UI Principles

### Roundedness

Our UI features subtle roundedness (1), offering a touch of softness without sacrificing a crisp, modern feel.

### Spacing
